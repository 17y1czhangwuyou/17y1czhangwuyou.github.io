Plot Synopsis

An ordinary high school girl Lin ZhenXin admires the most popular guy, OuYang FeiFan. While the school’s big bully, Xu TaiYu falls for the prettiest girl in the school, Tao MinMin. Lin ZhenXin and Xu TaiYu work together through many difficulties in order to get there dream date. Through their collaboration and as the day goes by,their friendship evolves and they learn more things about true love.
